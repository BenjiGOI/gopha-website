About GOPHA

The Global Optometry Public Health Alliance (GOPHA) is a non-profit organization that brings together optometry leaders from around the world. We work collectively to strengthen universal access to essential eye health services.

Global Team

Professionals from around the world working together

Our Vision

To create a future where everyone has access to comprehensive, high-quality eye care, ensuring lives free from preventable blindness and vision impairment.

Our Mission

To empower optometry professionals globally to alleviate the cause of preventable blindness and vision impairment, creating accessible and affordable eye care services for all.

Our Strategic Pillars

Four core areas that drive our mission to transform global eye health.

Global Advocacy

Drive policy to align with the World Health Assembly and UN resolutions on eye care, including collaboration with the WHO's SPECS 2030 initiative.

Workforce Development

Strengthen eye health capacity by supporting the development of optometry and its integration into national health systems.

Education & Awareness

Launch campaigns to educate professionals and the public on the importance of eye health and public health challenges.

Innovation

Promote the adoption of digital health solutions, such as tele-refraction, to expand services in underserved areas.

Membership Types

Choose the membership category that best fits your organization or professional background.

General Membership

Professional associations, academic institutions, and other entities directly involved in optometry.

Who Qualifies:
  • Professional optometry associations
  • Academic institutions with optometry programs
  • Organizations directly involved in optometry practice
  • Regulatory bodies for optometry

Associate Membership

Individuals or associations from countries where optometry is not legally recognized, and individuals providing primary eye care without formal recognition.

Who Qualifies:
  • Individuals from countries without optometry recognition
  • Associations in regions without regulatory frameworks
  • Primary eye care providers without formal optometry recognition
  • Organizations working toward optometry recognition

Affiliate Membership

Individuals, organizations, or institutions supporting GOPHA's vision and mission but not directly involved in optometry.

Who Qualifies:
  • Public health experts and organizations
  • Healthcare industry representatives
  • Non-governmental organizations (NGOs)
  • Other professionals interested in advancing eye health
