About GOPHA

The Global Optometry Public Health Alliance (GOPHA) brings together optometry leaders from around the world to collectively strengthen universal access to essential eye health services. We champion collaboration, advocacy, and innovation to ensure no one is left behind.

Our Core Strategic Pillars

Advocacy

We champion policies and partnerships that integrate eye health into broader public health agendas globally.

Workforce Development

We support the training and empowerment of eye care professionals in underserved communities.

Education

We provide resources and platforms for continuous learning and knowledge sharing in public eye health.

Innovation

We foster and promote innovative models of eye care delivery to reach the most vulnerable populations.
