About Our Global Optometry Public Health Alliance

The Global Optometry Public Health Alliance (GOPHA) is a non-profit organization that brings together optometry leaders from around the world. We work collectively to strengthen universal access to essential eye health services.

Global Team

Professionals from around the world working together

Our Vision

To create a future where everyone has access to comprehensive, high-quality eye care, ensuring lives free from preventable blindness and vision impairment.

Our Mission

To empower optometry professionals globally to alleviate the cause of preventable blindness and vision impairment, creating accessible and affordable eye care services for all.

Our Strategy for Global Eye Health

Discover the four core strategies we use to advance eye care as a public health priority around the world.

Global Advocacy

Drive policy to align with the World Health Assembly and UN resolutions on eye care, including collaboration with the WHO's SPECS 2030 initiative.

Workforce Development

Strengthen eye health capacity by supporting the development of optometry and its integration into national health systems.

Education & Awareness

Launch campaigns to educate professionals and the public on the importance of eye health and public health challenges.

Innovation

Promote the adoption of digital health solutions, such as tele-refraction, to expand services in underserved areas.

Membership Categories

General Membership

Open to optometry professional associations directly involved in the optometry field.

Rights & Benefits:
  • Eligible to vote at General Assembly
  • Can hold leadership positions

Associate Membership

An invitational category for associations from countries where primary eye care providers are not yet legally recognised as optometrists.

Rights:
  • May participate in projects
  • No voting rights
  • Cannot hold leadership positions

Affiliate Membership

For individuals, organisations, and academic institutions who support GOPHA's mission but do not qualify for General Membership.

Rights:
  • Includes individual optometrists, students, NGOs, & industry partners
  • Single representative voting rights
  • Position on executive committee

Benjamin Chandra

Research Associate

  • Benji Chandra serves as an Associate Researcher with the Global Optometry Public Health Alliance (GOPHA), where he is currently the global study coordinator for the global optometry education mapping study.
  • Currently living in the UK, Benji works as a Senior University Practitioner at the University of Huddersfield. He combines academic rigour with extensive clinical experience gained in specialist UK hospital clinics.
  • His passion for global public health was cemented during educational initiatives in Belize, Uganda, and India, where he witnessed firsthand that empowering local professionals through education is the most powerful force for sustainable change.
  • In addition to his research, Benji is the Head of Clinical Education at the Global Ophthalmic Institute and actively explores the use of generative AI to enhance education and productivity.
  • Outside of work you can find him going to church, recording a podcast, learning chess, or rock climbing.
