Membership

Join our global network and actively shape the future of optometry and vision equity.

Join Our Global Optometry Alliance

Joining GOPHA means more than becoming a member of an organisation; it means actively shaping the future of our profession and advancing vision equity for populations worldwide.

Membership Benefits

Amplify Your Voice & Influence Policy

Contribute to a powerful, unified voice advocating for optometry within global health systems. You will help shape policies that advance vision equity and align with WHO strategies like SPECS 2030.

Collaborate with a Global Network

Join an influential network of peers, thought leaders, and institutions. Forge partnerships, share high-impact strategies, and drive collective action on the world’s most pressing eye health challenges.

Advance Your Professional Expertise

Access specialized education and evidence-based best practices. Elevate your standing through unique opportunities to publish, present your work, and gain recognition on an international stage.

Drive High-Impact Research

Engage in multinational research collaborations. You will be part of driving innovation in scalable, community-based models of eye care that deliver tangible outcomes to inform policy.

Create Lasting Impact

Join a mission-driven community committed to results. Translate your expertise into action to strengthen health systems and create a world with better vision and better health for all.

Membership Categories

General Membership

Open to optometry professional associations directly involved in the optometry field.

Rights & Benefits:
  • Eligible to vote at General Assembly
  • Can hold leadership positions

Associate Membership

An invitational category for associations from countries where primary eye care providers are not yet legally recognised as optometrists.

Rights:
  • May participate in projects
  • No voting rights
  • Cannot hold leadership positions

Affiliate Membership

For individuals, organisations, and academic institutions who support GOPHA's mission but do not qualify for General Membership.

Rights:
  • Includes individual optometrists, students, NGOs, & industry partners
  • Single representative voting rights
  • Position on executive committee
